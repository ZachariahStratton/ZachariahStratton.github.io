SkwerlHurler
About

SkwerlHurler was a Unity Engine based mobile came created by myself and four other students at Texas A&M. It served as a practice point for team development, deadline scheduling, and proper communication handling for many of us. We created it with a focus on younger audiences and not only did it go over very well with our professor but his children couldn't put it down.

My Work

I took on the role of Art & Animation director for the game. This was something I was very unfamiliar with, but wanted to learn so I accepted the position. Nearly all of the visuals and animations in the game were created personally by me, bar a few assets we purchased for use to save time.

While this was a great challenge of a project for me, it also helped me to learn immensely. I also became very familiar with Unity game engine and C# coding in general.
