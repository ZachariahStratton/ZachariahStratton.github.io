Apocalypsis
About

Apocalyptica is a passion project created by myself and four other students at Texas A&M. It is a narrative-driven RPG created within the RPG Maker MV Engine.

This game was created with the intent to focus on a branching narrative and the challenges that come along with team-based project undergone by many people, each with their own independant vision of how the product should be. We wanted to work on our communication, participation, and compromising skills throughout the project development.

My Work

When developing this game, I lead the project and created the overall structure and workplan for all members. We chose a "Nexus" based story structure that I personally developed which allowed all of us to work on our own section of the game without encroaching on other's work.

My work focused on my storyline, an adventure into a dominant empire with a dark secret.
